Ph.D. student at IIT Kharagpur · Advised by Prof. Ayan Chowdhury

My research focuses on weakly supervised 3D plant point cloud segmentation, with the goal of developing data-efficient methods for plant part segmentation. I work on combining self-supervised representation learning, clustering, and prototype-based matching to build segmentation pipelines that can generalize across plant species with limited annotations.

I work with 3D plant datasets such as Pheno4D, PLANesT-3D, Arabidopsis, and ROSE-X, with applications in plant phenotyping and 3D computer vision.
